About Lana Exteriors

Founded in 2010, Lana Exteriors began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with superior exterior renovation services through specialized expertise and unwavering commitment to quality. Our journey started with a small team of dedicated craftsmen and has grown into a full-service exterior renovation company trusted by hundreds of homeowners across the region.

Our core values of integrity, expertise, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in using only the highest quality materials, employing specialized crews for each type of installation, and maintaining clear communication throughout every project. This dedication to excellence has earned us a reputation as one of the most trusted names in exterior home improvements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve the greater Alberta area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. Our installations typically come with a 10-year workmanship warranty, while manufacturer warranties on materials can extend up to 25 years or more.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Window installations typically take 1-3 days, siding projects 5-10 days, and roof replacements 2-4 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible financing options with competitive rates. We can discuss payment plans during your consultation.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We maintain all required licenses, bonds, and insurance coverage, including comprehensive liability insurance and WCB-Alberta coverage for all our workers.
